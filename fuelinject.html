GM Muscle: Types of Fuel Injection

Central Port Fuel Injection uses tubes with poppet valves from a central injector to spray fuel at each intake port rather than the central throttle-body.

Multipoint Fuel Injection injects fuel into the intake ports just upstream of each cylinder's intake valve rather than at a central point in an intake manifold.

Direct Fuel Injection injects fuel into the combustion chamber as opposed to injection before the intake valve in gasoline engines.
